What Is A Sports Official?

A sports official is a person who presides over a competitive athletic or sporting event to help maintain the standard of play.


Sports officials are also known as umpires, referees, or judges, depending on the sport. Some sports that require officials are listed below.

  • Basketball
  • Baseball/Softball
  • Football
  • Soccer
  • Volleyball

Responsibilities of Sports Officials

Sports officials are responsible for maintaining the integrity of the game while also ensuring the safety or all participants, including but not limited to, players, coaches, and game personnel. Some of the responsibilities are listed below.

  1. Extensive knowledge of the rules and mechanics of the sport
  2. Efficient game management
  3. Clean, professional appearance
  4. Effectively communicate with players, coaches, and game personnel
